Donut Storage Tips

  • Store donuts in an airtight container at room temperature to keep them soft and fresh.
  • Avoid refrigeration unless necessary — cold air dries donuts and cracks the glaze.
  • Place wax paper between layers of frosted or filled donuts to prevent sticking.
  • For a quick refresh, microwave a plain donut for 8-10 seconds to bring back warmth.
  • If you prefer a crisp texture, reheat donuts in the oven at low heat for a few minutes.
  • Always consume cream-filled donuts within 1–2 days for best taste and safety.
  • Freeze leftover donuts by wrapping them tightly; thaw and warm before serving.
